The following are movies we have watched.

We have rated them by each scoring each film on a scale of -5 to +5,
then summing our scores, adding 10, and dividing by 2.

  • Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery
    • Review: Brilliant spoof on the James Bond spy movie
    Rating: 9/10

  • Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me
    • Review: A sequel which dares to topple the original
    Rating: 9/10

  • Baby Driver
    • Review: Basic plot makes room for memorable characters and nonstop ear candy for our audio nerdery
    Rating: 9.5/10

  • Borat! Cultural Learnings of America for Make Benefit Glorious Nation of Kazakhstan
    • Review: Genius satire putting America on blast
    Rating: 9.5/10

  • Crazy Rich Asians
    • Review: Max's guilty pleasure, feel-good movie; Ali was not impressed
    Rating: 5.5/10

  • The Dawn Wall
    • Review: Riveting and inspiring on all counts
    Rating: 10/10

  • Doctor Strange
    • Review: Ali loved it but Max had some complaints about the limitations of magic
    Rating: 8.5/10

  • Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness
    • Review: Stylization and creator vision makes up for moments of cringe, but Ali thought it was scary
    Rating: 9/10

  • Dumb and Dumber
    • Review: Max thinks its hilarious but Ali thought it was gross
    Rating: 4.5/10

  • Elf
    • Review: A wholesome holiday classic featuring Will Farrell at his best
    Rating: 8.5/10

  • Everything Everywhere All at Once
    • Review: Well-crafted film managing an ambitious story and delivering strong representation
    Rating: 9.5/10

  • Ferris Bueller's Day Off
    • Review: The movie most vicariously lived-through by humanity
    Rating: 10/10

  • Groundhog Day
    • Review: This classic particularly spoke to Ali
    Rating: 9.5/10

  • Interstellar
    • Review: Perfect visuals, score, and stirring performances
    Rating: 10/10

  • Kung Fu Panda
    • Review: Strong, focused themes, stirring moments, fantastic sountrack, and engaging performances
    Rating: 10/10

  • The Matrix
    • Review: A perfect, visionary, intelligent film maximizing both style and substance
    Rating: 10/10

  • The Matrix Reloaded
    • Review: Just kept getting worse and worse
    Rating: 1/10

  • The Matrix Revolutions
    • Review: A bit more enjoyable than Reloaded, but still flawed with little substance
    Rating: 4/10

  • Moana
    • Review: Max loves the movie but Ali was bored by it
    Rating: 7/10

  • Office Space
    • Review: Speaks to the cynical worker in us all
    Rating: 8/10

  • The Princess and the Frog
    • Review: A wholesome adventure with good character arcs and music
    Rating: 10/10

  • The Room
    • Review: Absolute genius on display throughout this timeless masterpiece
    Rating: 10/10

  • Sherlock Holmes
    • Review: A fun and thrilling adventure with compelling stylization
    Rating: 9/10

  • Spider-Man: No Way Home

    Rating: 4/10

  • Star Wars: Episode 1 - The Phantom Menace
    • Review: Ali's favorite because of podracing and watching Young Anakin, but too many politics for Max

  • Star Wars: Episode 2 - Attack of the Clones
    • Review: We like the mood it puts us in even though its cringey
    Rating: 8.5/10

  • Star Wars: Episode 3 - Revenge of the Sith
    • Review: The stylization of the prequels speaks more to Ali than the originals

  • Star Wars: Episode 4 - A New Hope

  • Star Wars: Episode 5 - The Empire Strikes Back

    Rating: 9.5/10
